Math and Science

Hate running all over the calculator to find different menus? Well, now you can create your own customized Menu System Tree. The Custom Menu seems limited once you've tinkered around with these examples. There's no limit to the application of these in Math and Science classes. Have an entire System branch out into your different classes!

Systems

You can create full featured Menu Trees just like TI-OS. Have one on the bottom like the Custom Menu or branch that one out into other Menu Trees. Ever wanted to create a menu with conversions, constants, program names, and much more? You can do it!
