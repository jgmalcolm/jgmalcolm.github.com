The building is called Spire Midtown. The map below shows a green star for the building and visitor parking. If this is full, additional parking is available at the yellow star or on side streets.

You can enter the building from inside the parking deck (entrance on the 2nd level), or by the Peachtree St main entrance. There's a concierge who will buzz you up to the 21st floor, and my unit is #2103.

From Emory. Take Ponce de Leon heading West toward downtown. Take a right onto Peachtree St. Take a left on 7th street (Starbucks will be on your right as you turn), and park in the visitor deck on your left.

From I75 heading south. Take exit 250 for 10th/14th/17th street. Pass through 17th and stay in the left lane to take a left onto 10th. In three blocks, take a right onto Peachtree St. Take a right onto 7th street (Starbucks will be on your left as you turn), and park in the visitor deck on your left.
