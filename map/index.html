The building is called Spire Midtown at the corner of Peachtree & 7th. Google maps

MARTA. The Midtown Station (N4) is about a 5 minute walk. Exit the station on the side of Peachtree Place NW. Take a left out of the station heading toward Peachtree St. Take a right onto Peachtree St heading toward 860 Peachtree St.

From Emory. Take Ponce de Leon heading West toward downtown. Take a right onto Peachtree St. Take a left on 7th street (Starbucks will be on your right as you turn), and park in the visitor deck on your left.

From I75 heading south. Take exit 250 for 10th/14th/17th street. Pass through 17th and stay in the left lane to take a left onto 10th. In three blocks, take a right onto Peachtree St. In three blocks, take a right onto 7th street (Starbucks will be on your left as you turn), and park in the visitor deck on your left.

The map below shows a green star for the building which has pay parking on the bottom two floors. Free parking is available on Juniper St on a first-come, first-served basis. There are also two pay lots at the corners of Peachtree & 7th and Peachtree & 6th.

You can enter the building from inside the parking deck (entrance on the 2nd level), or by the Peachtree St main entrance. The concierge will buzz you up to the 21st floor, and my unit is #2103.
